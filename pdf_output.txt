 Kitchen Design –
This modular kitchen is designed with a soothing
combination of sage green and creamy white, reflecting
elegance, freshness, and functionality. The cabinetry is
crafted using HDHMR for durability and longevity, paired
with glossy acrylic shutters for a sleek, modern
appearance. The layout is thoughtfully planned to ensure
smooth workflow between the cooking, preparation, and
washing zones. Wicker baskets add a natural touch while

palette of muted greens and whites creates a calm and
airy ambiance, perfectly balanced with warm lighting and
minimal hardware. The design highlights both practicality
and style, offering a modern kitchen that blends texture,
tone, and technology seamlessly.

WARDROBES INTERNAL AND EXTERNAL DETAILING
CHILDREN BED 2D-DETAILING
The 2D detailing of the children’s bedroom focuses on creating a lively, imaginative, and functional space tailored to a child’s
needs and comfort. The layout is designed to ensure safe circulation, playful aesthetics, and efficient use of space. Each element
—from the bed placement to storage and study zones—is carefully planned for balance and usability. The color palette features
cheerful hues and contrasting tones that stimulate creativity and positivity. Ceiling and wall detailing incorporate layered,
colorful lighting to bring energy and warmth into the space. The electrical layout highlights the use of cove lights,
profile strips, and focus lights that enhance both mood and function. Detailed elevations and sectional drawings showcase
furniture design, material finishes, and joinery specifications for precise execution. The combination of soft furnishings,
wallpapers, and accent colors creates a joyful yet calming atmosphere. Every detail is aligned with the theme of fun, comfort,
and growth. These 2D drawings reflect not only technical accuracy but also a deep understanding of how design can shape a
child’s imagination and daily experience.
STUDY UNIT 2D-DETAILING
The 2D detailing of the study unit highlights a perfect balance between functionality, comfort, and
aesthetic design. The plan, elevation, and sectional drawings clearly define the layout, proportions, and
spatial organization of the unit. The design focuses on creating a productive workspace with ample storage
and display options. Shelves, drawers, and cabinets are arranged ergonomically to support efficient use of
space while maintaining a clean visual flow. The detailing emphasizes material finishes, edge profiles, and
joinery details for precision and durability. Lighting points are strategically positioned to ensure optimal
task lighting, preventing glare and shadow during study hours. The elevation showcases a structured
composition with balanced symmetry and refined lines, giving the unit a modern, elegant appearance. The
section drawing provides insight into depth, layering, and material thickness for accurate execution. A
soft, neutral color palette paired with subtle textures adds sophistication while promoting focus and
calmness. Overall, the 2D detailing demonstrates a thoughtful integration of design, function, and
technical accuracy, resulting in a study space that inspires creativity and concentration.
MASTER BEDROOM BED 2D-DETAILING
This master bedroom project showcases two distinct 2D design concepts that emphasize both functionality and aesthetic balance.
Each layout explores different arrangements of furniture, wardrobe placement, and circulation to optimize space utilization. The 2D
detailing includes the floor plan, elevations, and sectional drawings, highlighting clear dimensions and material specifications. One
design focuses on a sleek and modern composition with minimal lines, emphasizing open space and smooth finishes. The other
introduces a warmer, layered approach with textured surfaces, decorative panels, and accent elements. Both variations integrate
wardrobe and dressing areas strategically to enhance convenience and privacy. Detailed annotations specify joinery details, lighting
positions, and furniture proportions for execution accuracy. The drawings also include electrical and ceiling points aligned with the
furniture layout, ensuring a cohesive design outcome. Through these two 2D approaches, the project reflects creativity, technical
clarity, and adaptability—showcasing how a single space can transform through different design perspectives and material palettes.
The 2D detailing of the master bedroom focuses on a luxurious yet balanced composition of the bed and its background wall. The
bed is centrally aligned to create symmetry, with accurate dimensions marked for headboard height and bedside clearances. The
bed background features layered panel detailing, combining wooden textures, fabric padding, and profile lighting for a sophisticated
ambiance. Wall grooves and laminate patterns are illustrated with precision to enhance visual depth. The headboard design
integrates both aesthetics and comfort, supported by neatly proportioned side tables. LED strip lighting and wall sconces are
shown in plan and elevation for mood illumination. Electrical points for lamps and switches are clearly indicated. Material
annotations specify finishes like veneer, PU paint, and upholstered panels. Overall, the 2D drawing provides a complete visual and
dimensional guide for execution, ensuring perfect alignment between design intent and site implementation.
